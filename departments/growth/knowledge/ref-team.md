# Growth, Sales & New Markets Team Structure

Three related commercial functions under the Chief Commercial Officer (Casey Bartolucci), plus the New Markets team which reports directly to the CEO. Together they cover revenue operations, pipeline management, marketing, new business development, and market expansion.

## Reporting Structure

```text
Casey Bartolucci (CCO) → reports to Robby Knight (CEO)
  ├── Jared Childs (VP Growth)
  │     └── Alex Kochanik (Senior Manager, Growth)
  ├── Trish Lin (Sr. Director, Revenue Operations)
  ├── Jacob Murphy (VP Sales — Medicaid focus)
  ├── Reid Smith (Sr. Sales Manager)
  ├── Becca Cesario (Associate Sales Executive)
  ├── Jon Sayer (Capture Strategies Consultant — Contractor, RFX)
  └── Julie Fleischer (CXO — Member Experience, separate dept)

Sarah Hagan (GM, New Markets) → reports to Robby Knight (CEO)
  ├── Julie Nguyen (Senior Director)
  ├── Lara Lasic (Associate Director)
  └── Mason Joyner (Senior Manager)

Ale Palmer (Associate Manager, SNAP/EBT) → reports to Daryl Risinger (President)
```

## Growth

Revenue operations, marketing, and growth strategy.

### Jared Childs -- VP Growth

- **Role**: Leads the Growth function — marketing strategy, retailer partnerships, revenue growth initiatives
- **Key channels**: `#growth-team`
- **Reports to**: Casey Bartolucci (CCO)

### Trish Lin -- Sr. Director, Revenue Operations

- **Role**: Leads revenue operations — CRM management, pipeline analytics, go-to-market process, reporting
- **Key channels**: `#growth-team`
- **Reports to**: Casey Bartolucci (CCO)

### Alex Kochanik -- Senior Manager, Growth

- **Role**: Marketing execution — retailer partnerships, in-store media programs, CPG brand deals
- **Key channels**: `#growth-team`
- **Reports to**: Jared Childs (VP Growth)

## Sales

Drives new business across Medicare Advantage, Medicaid, and emerging commercial markets.

### Casey Bartolucci -- Chief Commercial Officer

- **Role**: Department head for Sales, Growth, and Member Experience. Owns overall commercial strategy, revenue targets, and go-to-market.
- **Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)

### Jacob Murphy -- VP Sales

- **Role**: Leads sales with a focus on Medicaid opportunities. Manages prospect relationships, pipeline, and deal execution.
- **Key channels**: Prospect-specific channels (`#prospect-*`)
- **Reports to**: Casey Bartolucci (CCO)

### Reid Smith -- Sr. Sales Manager

- **Role**: Sales execution — prospect outreach, pipeline management, deal support
- **Reports to**: Casey Bartolucci (CCO)

### Becca Cesario -- Associate Sales Executive

- **Role**: Sales support and business development
- **Reports to**: Casey Bartolucci (CCO)

### Jon Sayer -- Capture Strategies Consultant (Contractor)

- **Role**: RFP/RFI response management — drafting proposals, managing the RFX process, coordinating cross-functional input for bids
- **Reports to**: Casey Bartolucci (CCO)

## New Markets

Explores and executes expansion into markets beyond current Medicare Advantage supplemental benefits. Primary focus areas: SNAP/EBT, Medicaid, and state-level government programs.

### Sarah Hagan -- GM, New Markets

- **Role**: Leads the New Markets function. Owns federal EBT/SNAP market development (USDA/FNS engagement), state government implementations, RFP/RFI responses for new program types, and board-level strategic reporting.
- **Key workstreams**: Federal EBT/SNAP (FNS/USDA proposals), state implementations (Iowa, Nebraska, Idaho, Ohio), government RFP/RFI response, board and strategic planning
- **Key channels**: `#prospect-ebt-nebraska-hhs`, `#prospect-ebt-iowa-hhs`, `#ebt-payments`, `#prospect-ebt-ohio-djfs`
- **Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)

### Julie Nguyen -- Senior Director

- **Role**: Senior strategy and operations for New Markets — supports federal engagement, state expansion planning, and cross-functional coordination
- **Reports to**: Sarah Hagan

### Lara Lasic -- Associate Director

- **Role**: New Markets operations — state implementation support, regulatory research, proposal development
- **Reports to**: Sarah Hagan

### Mason Joyner -- Senior Manager

- **Role**: New Markets execution — state program engagement, implementation logistics, market research
- **Reports to**: Sarah Hagan

### Ale Palmer -- Associate Manager, SNAP/EBT

- **Role**: Focused on SNAP/EBT program operations and execution
- **Reports to**: Daryl Risinger (President)
