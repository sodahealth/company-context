# Evermore Leadership Team

Evermore (formerly Soda Health) is a ~100-person company building the infrastructure
behind supplemental health benefits — the technology that approves transactions in real
time when Medicare members use their benefit cards. The company serves health plans as
customers and their members as end users.

## Key Cross-Functional Programs

### OKR Process

Evermore runs quarterly OKRs across all departments. The Universal Roadmap in Jira
(managed by Product) and the OKR tracker (managed by Chief of Staff) are the primary
sources of truth. Department heads own their OKRs; the COO role is expected to help
with cross-department OKR alignment and execution accountability.

### Company Operating Rhythm

- **All-hands:** Monthly, run by the CEO. Major updates, wins, and strategic direction.
- **Leadership sync:** Weekly, CEO + direct reports. Run by Chief of Staff.
- **Department reviews:** Quarterly, each department head presents to leadership.

### Hiring

Evermore is actively hiring across Engineering, Growth, and HPS. The open COO role
was the most significant hire underway (now filled). Hiring is managed through
Greenhouse and coordinated by People Ops (Regina Lindsey) with each hiring manager.

## Company Context

### The Name

The company is mid-rebrand from **Soda Health** → **evermore outcomes**. Both names
are in active use. Email addresses use `@sodahealth.com`; the public brand is
evermore / `@evermoreoutcomes.com`. When in doubt, `sodahealth.com` works for all
systems access.

### The Product

- **Harmony API** — Real-time transaction processing for supplemental health benefits.
  Approves or denies transactions when a member uses their benefit card at a merchant.
- **&more** — The member-facing brand for the benefit card program.
- **Sponsor Hub** — Health plan portal for configuring benefit programs.
- **CSRX** — Internal tool used by the Care team for member support and transaction lookups.

### Who We Serve

- **Health plans** (sponsors) are the customers — they purchase our platform to administer
  supplemental benefits for their members.
- **Medicare Advantage members** are the end users — they use &more cards to buy groceries,
  OTC products, and other eligible items.
- **Merchants** accept the cards at point of sale.
