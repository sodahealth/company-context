## Key Systems Used by Care

### CSRX (Primary Internal Tool)

CSRX is Evermore's internal customer care tooling platform. It is the primary system used by Care agents and managers for member support -- account lookups, issue resolution, benefit inquiries, card status checks, and case management. CSRX adoption and process improvement is owned by the Care team.

### Partner Help Center

The Partner Help Center is the externally-facing knowledge base and support portal for retail partners and members. Customer Care owns the content, FAQs, and help articles. Desiree NeuPhresh manages content updates and knowledge base intakes.

### Call Center / Telephony Platform

The call center telephony platform handles inbound member calls, IVR routing, warm transfers, and workforce scheduling. Care manages agent queues, call routing rules, and scheduling.

### Jira

Used for escalation tracking and cross-functional ticket workflows. Care creates and manages tickets for sponsor escalations, BOOST requests, and CTM tracking. Key project areas include care-related BOOST tickets and escalation workflows.

### Slack

Primary communication tool for team coordination and sponsor-level discussions.

| Channel | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `#care-team` | Care team coordination and updates |
| `#coco-meeting-live-programs-collaboration` | Cross-functional live programs coordination |
| `#internal-<sponsor>` | Per-sponsor channels for escalations and daily coordination |

### Sponsor Hub

Client-facing portal used by sponsors to manage their benefits programs. Customer Success and Account Management reference Sponsor Hub data when discussing program health and reporting with sponsors.

### CHIME (Power BI)

Internal analytics platform for Medicare enrollment data. Customer Success uses CHIME dashboards and reports for sponsor reporting and program health reviews.
