# Care Tools

Tools and integrations used by the Customer Care and Customer Success teams.

## Primary Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Used By |
|------|---------|---------|
| CSRX | Member support -- account lookups, issue resolution, benefit inquiries, card status, case management | Customer Care (daily) |
| Partner Help Center | External knowledge base and support portal for members and retail partners | Customer Care (content management) |
| Call center / telephony | Inbound call handling, IVR routing, warm transfers, workforce scheduling | Customer Care (daily) |
| Jira | Escalation tracking, BOOST tickets, CTM tracking, cross-functional workflows | Care and CS |
| Slack | Team coordination, sponsor-channel communication, escalation alerts | All Care/CS |
| Sponsor Hub | Client-facing portal for sponsors to manage benefits programs | Customer Success, Account Management |
| CHIME (Power BI) | Medicare enrollment analytics, sponsor reporting dashboards | Customer Success |
| Outlook | Email communication with sponsors, scheduling | All Care/CS |
| Smartsheet | Tracking and project management for sponsor programs | Customer Success |

## Integrations

### CSRX and the Call Center

CSRX is the primary screen Care agents use during member calls. It integrates with the telephony platform so agents can look up member accounts, check benefit balances, review transaction history, and resolve issues while on a call.

### Jira and Slack

Escalations raised in Jira are often discussed in per-sponsor Slack channels (`#internal-<sponsor>`). CTM tickets and BOOST requests flow through Jira with notifications to relevant Slack channels.

### CHIME and Sponsor Reporting

Customer Success uses CHIME dashboards to prepare sponsor health reports and program reviews. Data from CHIME feeds into the reporting shared with sponsors during regular review meetings.

### Partner Help Center and CSRX

Care agents reference the Partner Help Center for standard answers and direct members to its self-service resources. Content updates to the Partner Help Center are coordinated through the Care team.
