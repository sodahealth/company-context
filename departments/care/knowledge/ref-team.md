# Care Team Structure

The Care department encompasses two sub-functions: **Customer Care** and **Customer Success** (including Account Management). Together they own the member support experience and the post-launch sponsor relationship at Evermore.

- **Customer Care** runs the member-facing call center and support operations -- call handling, agent training, workforce scheduling, quality assurance, Partner Help Center, and regulatory support (CTMs).
- **Customer Success** owns the strategic sponsor relationship after go-live -- program health, escalations, contract SLAs, and cross-functional coordination.
- **Account Management** handles day-to-day sponsor needs, reporting, and billing coordination.

## Team Members

### Customer Care

#### Desiree NeuPhresh -- Senior Director, Customer Care

- **Role**: Leads all customer care operations. Owns call center management, agent training programs, Partner Help Center content and processes, CTM (Complaints to Medicare) handling, and regulatory support interactions.
- **Key channels**: `#care-team`
- **Reports to**: Ariane Grazian (SVP, Health Plan Solutions)

#### Patrice James -- Senior Manager, Customer Care

- **Role**: Supports call center operations, workforce scheduling, quality assurance, and day-to-day care team management.
- **Reports to**: Desiree NeuPhresh

### Customer Success

#### Micheline Tocco -- Head of Customer Success

- **Role**: Leads strategic sponsor relationships and program health. Owns sponsor escalation processes, SLA management, and cross-functional coordination for live programs.
- **Reports to**: Daryl Risinger (President)

#### Josie Baker -- Director, Customer Success

- **Role**: Manages Customer Success Managers. Handles benefit edge cases, BOOST ticket processes, and sponsor relationship management.
- **Reports to**: Daryl Risinger (President)

#### Genesis Coste -- Account Manager

- **Role**: Account management for assigned sponsors, including funding coordination.
- **Reports to**: Micheline Tocco

#### Jenny McClure -- Customer Success Manager

- **Reports to**: Josie Baker

#### Rayanna Floyd -- Sr. Customer Success Coordinator

- **Reports to**: Josie Baker

#### Shawnalyn Moore -- Customer Success Manager

- **Reports to**: Josie Baker

#### Viki Smith -- Customer Success Manager

- **Reports to**: Julie Fleischer (CXO) -- cross-department reporting

### Account Management

#### Rachel McMillan -- Director of Account Management

- **Role**: Leads account management function. Handles sponsor stewardship, day-to-day sponsor needs, and customer management.
- **Reports to**: Micheline Tocco (Head of Customer Success)

#### Kaity Galanos -- Account Manager

- **Role**: Day-to-day sponsor account management, reporting, and billing coordination. Manages post-implementation relationships for sponsors including SCAN CA, SFHP, HSJMC, and CalOptima.
- **Reports to**: Rachel McMillan
