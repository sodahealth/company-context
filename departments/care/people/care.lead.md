# Desiree NeuPhresh

**Title**: Senior Director, Customer Care

**Department**: Care

**Reports to**: Ariane Grazian (SVP, Health Plan Solutions)

## Role

Desiree NeuPhresh leads Customer Care at Evermore. She owns the member-facing call center and all support operations, ensuring members receive timely, accurate, and compassionate help with their benefits.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Call center operations** -- Manages the call center, including agent staffing, workforce scheduling, and daily operations
- **Agent training** -- Owns training programs for care agents, including onboarding new agents and ongoing skill development
- **Partner Help Center** -- Manages the external knowledge base and support portal content, FAQs, and help articles for members and retail partners
- **CTM handling** -- Oversees Complaints to Medicare (CTM) processes, ensuring regulatory compliance and timely resolution
- **Quality assurance** -- Drives QA programs for call quality, member satisfaction, and care process improvement
- **CSRX adoption** -- Champions adoption and effective use of the CSRX internal care tooling platform
- **New launch readiness** -- Coordinates care team readiness for new product launches and sponsor go-lives (IVR routing, warm transfer protocols, knowledge base intakes)
