# HPS Team Structure

Health Plan Solutions (HPS) manages the implementation and ongoing relationship lifecycle for health plan sponsor clients. The team handles new client implementations (eligibility files, benefit configuration, testing, go-live), renewals, and day-to-day operational partnership.

## Reporting Structure

Ariane Grazian (SVP, Health Plan Solutions) reports to Robby Knight (CEO).

```text
Ariane Grazian (SVP, Health Plan Solutions)
  ├── Tal Topf (Associate Director -- Implementation Lead)
  ├── Diane Borton (Senior Manager)
  ├── Megan Guillory (Senior Manager)
  ├── Jordan Savold (Associate Director)
  └── Cori Billings (Associate Director, Implementations)
```

Additionally, Customer Care (Desiree NeuPhresh, Patrice James) reports to Ariane.

## Team Members

### Ariane Grazian -- SVP, Health Plan Solutions

- **Role**: Department head. Owns overall implementation operations, benefit design, retail network strategy, SOW/contracting, and customer escalations.
- **Key channels**: `#sponsor-implementations`, customer-specific channels
- **Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)
- **Also manages**: Customer Care team

### Tal Topf -- Associate Director, Implementation Lead

- **Role**: Overall implementation coordination and process design. Owns OnRamp workflows, implementation milestones, risk management, and the master task inventory.
- **Focus areas**:
  - Implementation process architecture and automation roadmap
  - OnRamp workflows and the Osprey replacement initiative
  - Implementation milestone tracking and risk management
  - RFP/sales support with implementation expertise
  - Team retrospectives and process improvement
- **Key channels**: `#sponsor-implementations`, `#implementation-2dot0`
- **Daily tools**: Outlook (primary interface), Smartsheet (PM backbone), Auth0 (product access), SharePoint (document management)
- **Customer portfolio**: Manages multiple parallel implementations; broad, shallow engagement across all active customers
- **Reports to**: Ariane Grazian

### Diane Borton -- Senior Manager

- **Role**: Implementation Manager handling customer-facing implementation calls, OnRamp module management, and materials coordination.
- **Customer portfolio**: SCAN Health Plan (SCN), SummaCare (SMC)
- **Focus areas**: OnRamp module management, customer-facing implementation calls, materials coordination
- **Reports to**: Ariane Grazian

### Megan Guillory -- Senior Manager

- **Role**: Implementation Manager focused on new implementations and eligibility processes.
- **Customer portfolio**: Imperial Health Plan (IHP), San Francisco Health Plan (SFHP), HSJMC, Blackhawk, Sentara
- **Focus areas**: New implementations, eligibility processes, UAT tracking
- **Reports to**: Ariane Grazian

### Jordan Savold -- Associate Director

- **Role**: Implementation operations and strategy
- **Customer portfolio**: Braven Health / Horizon (BHZ)
- **Reports to**: Ariane Grazian

### Cori Billings -- Associate Director, Implementations

- **Role**: Implementation operations
- **Reports to**: Ariane Grazian
