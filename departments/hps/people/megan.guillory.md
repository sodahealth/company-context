# Megan Guillory

**Title**: Senior Manager, Health Plan Solutions

**Department**: Health Plan Solutions (HPS) -- Implementation

**Reports to**: Ariane Grazian (SVP, Health Plan Solutions)

## Role

Megan is an Implementation Manager (IM) focused on new implementations and eligibility processes. She handles the most active pipeline of new customer onboarding and manages UAT tracking and coordination.

## Key Responsibilities

- Managing end-to-end implementation workflows for new customer onboarding
- Eligibility file setup, testing, and sign-off coordination
- UAT tracking and coordination (built manual UAT process with tracking files)
- Customer-facing implementation calls and timeline management
- Materials coordination for new implementations
- Coordinating across internal teams for each customer
