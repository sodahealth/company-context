# Tal Topf

**Title**: Associate Director, Health Plan Solutions (referred to internally as VP HPS / Implementation Lead)

**Department**: Health Plan Solutions (HPS) -- Implementation

**Reports to**: Ariane Grazian (SVP, Health Plan Solutions)

## Role

Tal leads the implementation function at Evermore. She is responsible for overall implementation coordination, process design, and the implementation automation roadmap. She owns the OnRamp workflows and milestones, manages the master task inventory (~350+ tasks), and drives the Osprey initiative (the OnRamp replacement).

## Key Responsibilities

- Overall implementation coordination across all health plan sponsors
- Implementation process architecture and automation roadmap (the "Implementation 2.0" initiative)
- OnRamp workflow management and the Osprey replacement initiative
- Implementation milestone tracking and risk management
- Maintaining the master implementation task inventory
- RFP and sales support with implementation expertise and timeline estimates
- Team retrospectives and process improvement
- Recruiting for the implementation team

## Customer Portfolio

Tal manages multiple parallel implementations with a broad, supervisory engagement across all active customers. She does not typically own individual customer implementations day-to-day (that falls to Diane and Megan) but coordinates across all of them.
