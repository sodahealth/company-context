# Regina Lindsey

**Title**: Head of People and Performance

**Department**: People Operations

**Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)

**Email**: <regina.lindsey@evermoreoutcomes.com>

## Role

Regina leads People Operations at Evermore, owning the full employee lifecycle for a ~100-person company. She and Patricia Galvez (consultant) are a two-person team responsible for everything from recruiting and hiring through performance management and offboarding.

## Key Responsibilities

- Hiring -- job descriptions, recruiting pipeline, interview coordination, offer letters
- Onboarding -- new hire enrollment in Rippling, Jira HI project tracking, equipment ordering, orientation coordination with IT
- Offboarding -- separation processes, Rippling termination, Jira OFF project tracking, access revocation coordination
- Compensation and benefits -- comp bands, bonus frameworks (discretionary bonus payout), benefits administration
- Workforce planning -- headcount calibration, leveling frameworks, leadership leveling tenets
- Performance management -- the "evolve by evermore" program including 360 reviews, calibration cycles, and training
- Board and leadership reporting -- headcount metrics, people data for board presentations, OKR tracking
- Internal communications -- company-wide people announcements, policy updates, evolve program rollout

## Key Systems

- **Rippling** -- HRIS core (employee records, payroll, benefits, ATS) -- her most critical daily tool
- **Office 365** -- Outlook for email, SharePoint for documents, Forms for 360 reviews and surveys
- **Slack** -- Primary real-time communication (`#people-ops`)
- **Jira** -- HI project (onboarding) and OFF project (offboarding)
- **15Five** -- Performance management check-ins
- **Firstbase** -- Equipment procurement for new hires
- **Zoom** -- Candidate interviews and external meetings
- **Brex** -- Corporate expenses
