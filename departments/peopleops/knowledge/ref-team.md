# People Operations Team Structure

People Operations owns HR, hiring, onboarding/offboarding, compensation, and employee experience for all of Evermore. The team manages the full employee lifecycle -- from recruiting and offer letters through performance management and offboarding. With a two-person team supporting a ~100-person company, the department works closely with every department head and relies heavily on cross-functional partnerships to keep things running smoothly.

## Mission and Scope

- **Hiring** -- Job descriptions, recruiting pipeline, interview coordination, offer letters, and onboarding new hires
- **Onboarding** -- New hire orientation, Rippling enrollment, Jira HI project ticket tracking, IT provisioning coordination, equipment ordering (Firstbase)
- **Offboarding** -- Separation processes, Rippling termination, Jira OFF project tracking, access revocation coordination with IT
- **Compensation and Benefits** -- Comp bands, bonus frameworks, benefits administration, workforce planning and headcount calibration
- **Performance Management** -- The "evolve by evermore" performance program including 360 reviews, calibration cycles, and leveling frameworks
- **Employee Experience** -- Internal communications, policy guidance, leave management, name changes, and day-to-day HR support
