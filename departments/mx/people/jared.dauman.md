# Jared Dauman

**Title**: Chief Payments Officer / Head of Operations

**Department**: Merchants & Payments (MX)

**Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)

**UPN**: <jared.dauman@evermoreoutcomes.com>

## Role

Jared Dauman is a co-founder of Evermore and leads the Merchants & Payments function. As CPO and Head of Operations, he oversees the payment infrastructure that powers Evermore's prepaid benefit cards -- including FIS and Galileo integrations, merchant onboarding, settlement and reconciliation, funding flows, card operations, and the EBT/SNAP expansion.

Beyond M&P, Jared participates in company-wide operations as a co-founder: OKRs, board meetings, payroll oversight, and company policy governance on Confluence.

## Key Responsibilities

- Payment infrastructure strategy and operations (FIS, Galileo, settlement)
- Merchant onboarding and retail partner ecosystem management
- EBT/SNAP platform development and FNS compliance
- Financial operations and expense management (Brex)
- Company policy governance and institutional knowledge (Confluence power user)
- Sales and business development support (RFPs, RFIs, customer demos)
- Contract and SOW review for the payments function
