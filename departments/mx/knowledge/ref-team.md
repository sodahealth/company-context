# MX Department Team Structure

The MX department covers two sub-functions that together own the member-facing experience and the payment infrastructure that powers it:

- **Member Experience** -- Owns the member-facing brand, design, and communication experience. Manages the &more brand identity, visual design, in-store marketing materials, printed collateral (card carriers, catalogs), and cross-channel member engagement strategy.
- **Merchants & Payments** -- Manages the payment infrastructure: FIS and Galileo integrations, merchant onboarding, settlement/reconciliation, funding flows, card operations, and new payment rails (EBT).

## Team Members

### Merchants & Payments

#### Jared Dauman -- CPO / Head of Operations

- **Role**: Executive owner of Merchants & Payments. Co-founder. Oversees payment infrastructure, merchant onboarding, settlement, card operations, and EBT expansion. Also participates in company-wide operations as a co-founder (OKRs, board meetings, payroll oversight, company policies).
- **Key channels**: `#general-merchantsandpayments`, `#ebt-payments`, `#funding-account-alerts`, `#it-sec`
- **Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)

### Member Experience

#### Julie Fleischer -- CXO (Chief Experience Officer)

- **Role**: Department head for Member Experience. Owns the &more brand identity, member communications strategy, and the overall member-facing experience design. Reports to Casey Bartolucci (CCO).
- **Key channels**: `#cpg-pharmavite`, `#product-cpg-2026`, `#cpg-alcon`, `#internal-blackhawk`
- **Reports to**: Casey Bartolucci (CCO)
- **Also manages**: Viki Smith (Customer Success), Sydney Ringold (Sales) -- cross-department reports

#### Nidhi Nayyar -- Senior Manager, Member Experience

- **Role**: Manages the member experience execution -- package configuration, ABCorp coordination, kit setup, printed materials, and in-store marketing materials.
- **Key channels**: `#project-materials`
- **Reports to**: Ariane Grazian (SVP, HPS) -- dotted-line to Julie Fleischer

#### Ann Grafelman -- Visual Designer

- **Role**: Visual design for the &more brand -- card carriers, catalogs, in-store signage, digital assets, and member communications.
- **Reports to**: Nidhi Nayyar

#### Jill Blacketer -- Contract Designer

- **Role**: Contract design work supporting the &more brand and member materials.
- **Reports to**: Sarah Rumfelt (Chief of Staff)
