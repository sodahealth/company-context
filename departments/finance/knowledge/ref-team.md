# Finance & Legal Team Structure

The Finance department covers two sub-functions: Finance (accounting, invoicing, financial reporting) and Legal (contract review, regulatory compliance, legal operations). Both report to the CEO.

## Reporting Structure

```text
Robby Knight (CEO)
  ├── Brian Ru (Head of Corporate Development and Finance)
  │     ├── Zach Newcomb (Sr. Finance Manager)
  │     └── Shaun Rizzolo (Accounting Manager, CPA)
  └── Greg McNeal (General Counsel, Contractor)
        ├── Yahitza Nuñez (Senior Director of Compliance)
        ├── Ryan Rasdall (Associate Counsel)
        ├── Julio Melendez (Legal Ops Manager)
        └── Emma Days (Interim Associate General Counsel, Contractor)
```

## Finance Team (3 people)

### Brian Ru -- Head of Corporate Development and Finance

- **Role**: Department head. Owns accounting, invoicing, financial reporting, purchase orders, and corporate development.
- **Key channels**: `#finance`
- **Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)

### Zach Newcomb -- Sr. Finance Manager

- **Role**: Finance operations, sponsor invoicing (monthly billing cycles), financial reconciliation, and reporting.
- **Focus areas**: Sponsor billing cycle coordination with HPS, invoice generation, financial analysis
- **Key channels**: `#finance`
- **Reports to**: Brian Ru

### Shaun Rizzolo -- Accounting Manager, CPA

- **Role**: Accounting operations, AP/AR processing, print/materials cost tracking, general ledger maintenance.
- **Focus areas**: Bill.com invoice processing, vendor payments, cost tracking, month-end close
- **Key channels**: `#finance`
- **Reports to**: Brian Ru

## Legal Team (4 people)

### Greg McNeal -- General Counsel (Contractor)

- **Role**: Legal department head. Oversees contract review, compliance, and legal operations.
- **Key channels**: `#legal`
- **Reports to**: Robby Knight (CEO)

### Yahitza Nuñez -- Senior Director of Compliance

- **Role**: Leads compliance program across the organization.
- **Focus areas**: HIPAA compliance, TCPA/SMS regulations, WCAG accessibility requirements, state-specific regulatory requirements, audit responses, compliance training
- **Key channels**: `#legal`, `#compliance`
- **Reports to**: Greg McNeal

### Ryan Rasdall -- Associate Counsel

- **Role**: Contract review and vendor legal diligence.
- **Focus areas**: Vendor contracts, partnership agreements, NDA review, legal risk assessment, regulatory research
- **Key channels**: `#legal`
- **Reports to**: Greg McNeal

### Julio Melendez -- Legal Ops Manager

- **Role**: Legal operations, process management, and administrative coordination.
- **Focus areas**: Contract lifecycle management (Ironclad), legal request intake, document management, reporting
- **Key channels**: `#legal`
- **Reports to**: Greg McNeal

### Emma Days -- Interim Associate General Counsel (Contractor)

- **Role**: General legal support on a contract basis.
- **Reports to**: Greg McNeal
