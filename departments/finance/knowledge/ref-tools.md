# Finance & Legal Tools

Tools and integrations used by the Finance and Legal teams at Evermore.

## Finance Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Access Group |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Bill.com | AP/AR processing, vendor invoice management, payment approvals | Finance team (Bill.com account) |
| Office 365 -- Excel | Billing cycle spreadsheets, cost tracking, purchase order tracking, financial models | department-finance (Entra) |
| SharePoint | Financial document storage, shared workbooks, records retention | department-finance (Entra) |
| Outlook | Email and calendar -- billing coordination, vendor communications | department-finance (Entra) |

## Legal Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Access Group |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Ironclad | Contract lifecycle management -- creation, review, routing, e-signatures, storage | Legal team (Ironclad account) |
| Vanta | Compliance evidence collection, policy management, audit preparation (HIPAA, SOC 2) | Compliance + IT+SEC (Vanta account) |
| Office 365 -- Word | Contract drafting, legal memos, compliance documentation, policy authoring | department-finance (Entra) |
| SharePoint | Legal document library, executed contract archive, policy repository | department-finance (Entra) |
| Outlook | Email and calendar -- contract negotiations, compliance communications | department-finance (Entra) |

## Integrations

### Bill.com and Finance Workflows

- Vendor invoices are submitted and approved through Bill.com
- Print and materials cost tracking for sponsor kits flows through Bill.com
- Payment status is tracked for AP reconciliation

### Ironclad and Contract Workflows

- New contracts are routed through Ironclad for legal review and approval
- Executed contracts are stored in Ironclad with metadata for search and reporting
- Contract templates are maintained in Ironclad for common agreement types (NDAs, vendor agreements, SOWs)

### Vanta and Compliance

- Compliance evidence is collected automatically where possible (integrations with cloud services)
- Audit preparation workflows are managed through Vanta
- Policy documents are maintained and versioned in Vanta
- Shared ownership between Legal (Yahitza Nuñez) and IT+SEC (Umang Kapadia)
