# Evermore — Company Overview

## What Evermore Does

Evermore is a healthcare technology company focused on reducing health inequity. We provide "Smart Benefits" — prepaid Mastercard cards that connect Medicare Advantage and Medicaid members to over-the-counter (OTC) products, groceries, and health services at major retail partners like Walgreens, CVS, Kroger, and Walmart. Health plans hire Evermore to administer these supplemental benefits on behalf of their members. You will see two brand names: **&more** is the consumer-facing brand that members see on their cards and in the app, while **Evermore** is the business-to-business name used with health plan clients and partners.

## Who We Serve

- **Health plans (sponsors)** — Medicare Advantage and Medicaid organizations that contract with Evermore to offer supplemental benefit programs to their members. These are our paying clients.
- **Members (beneficiaries)** — The individuals enrolled in those health plans who receive an &more card and use it to access their benefits. They are the people we ultimately exist to help.
- **Merchants (retail partners)** — Stores and pharmacies where members shop using their &more cards. Our merchant network includes national chains and regional retailers across the country.

## Our Products

- **Harmony API** — The core technology platform that powers everything. It manages accounts, cards, benefits, funding, and transactions behind the scenes.
- **&more card and online experience** — The prepaid Mastercard that members use at retail stores, plus the mobile app and web portal where they can check balances, find eligible products, and manage their account.
- **Sponsor Hub** — A client-facing portal where health plans manage their benefits programs, view reports, and track member activity.
- **CSRX** — Internal tooling used by our Customer Care team to look up member information, troubleshoot issues, and provide support quickly.

## How the Company Is Organized

- **Product Engineering** — Builds and maintains all of Evermore's technology, from the core platform to the mobile app and internal tools.
- **Product** — Owns the product roadmap and decides what features get built and in what order.
- **Health Plan Solutions** — Manages implementation and ongoing relationships with health plan clients from contract signing through go-live and beyond.
- **Customer Success** — Ensures clients get value from the platform after launch, handling escalations and monitoring program health.
- **Customer Care** — Runs member-facing support operations, including the call center and care team.
- **Merchants & Payments** — Manages payment infrastructure, merchant onboarding, settlement, and card operations.
- **Growth** — Drives revenue operations, marketing, and go-to-market strategy.
- **Sales** — Brings in new business by responding to proposals and managing the sales pipeline across Medicare Advantage, Medicaid, and newer markets.
- **New Markets** — Explores expansion into areas beyond current Medicare Advantage benefits, including SNAP/EBT and state-level programs.
- **Member Experience** — Owns the &more brand, visual design, printed materials, and the overall member-facing experience.
- **Finance** — Manages accounting, invoicing, financial reporting, and billing.
- **Legal** — Handles contracts, regulatory compliance (including HIPAA), and legal operations.
- **People Operations** — Manages hiring, onboarding, compensation, and the employee experience.
- **Office of the CEO** — Coordinates company-wide strategic initiatives and executive operations.

## Key Numbers

- **Team size:** Approximately 99 people — 81 full-time employees and 18 contractors.
- **Banking partners:** Avidia Bank (primary issuing bank) and The Bancorp Bank, N.A. (legacy programs).
- **Card network:** Mastercard.
